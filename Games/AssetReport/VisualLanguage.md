# Asset Report — Visual Language (Creative DNA)

> The **concise creative DNA** of *Asset Report*, written to be loaded quickly
> before generating artwork. It **complements** the master
> [Fantasy Strategy style guide](../../StyleGuides/Fantasy-Strategy-v1.md) (which
> owns *how* art is rendered) and condenses the
> [Game Art Bible](GameArtBible.md) (which owns *what* this game is). If you read
> only one game-specific file, read this — then go deeper as needed.

## One-sentence identity

*Asset Report* is **heroic bureaucratic fantasy**: a warm, civilized
kingdom-management game where epic heroes, gods, and dragons are rendered in
total sincerity — and **you are the clerk who does their paperwork.**

## The core tension (never break it)

**Epic glory above, mundane ledger below.** Draw the fantasy *straight and
grand*; let the *framing* be wry. The comedy is juxtaposition (a god-king's
blessing stamped **DENIED** for a missing receipt), never silly rendering. If the
heroes ever look like a joke, the art has failed.

## Five pillars

1. **Heroic administration.** The player's fantasy is competence behind the
   throne. The desk, the ledger, the stamp, and the wax seal are protagonists,
   not chrome.
2. **Warm golden-hour realm.** A prosperous, civilized, late-afternoon world —
   gold light on weathered stone. Optimistic, never grimdark, never sterile.
3. **Civic blue-and-gold, military red, gold economy, radiant divine.** Color and
   banner tell you allegiance and function instantly (style-guide faction keys).
4. **Only the sacred glows.** Light is rationed to the divine and arcane; the
   whole mundane world is honest, non-emissive material. Wonder stays wonderful.
5. **One subject, strong silhouette, room to breathe.** Read it as a black shape
   first; readability beats detail every time (style-guide creative DNA).

## Signature visual system

- **The map node:** every building is a painterly isometric scene inside a
  **consistent gold Base Ring**, with a **circular gold Top Badge** (or **red
  heraldic shield** for military/defense) and a warm **epic rim light**. The
  furniture never changes; only the building does. → [`Buildings.md`](Buildings.md)
- **Two badge frames carry category:** circular gold (resource / civic / economy /
  research / divine) vs. red heraldic shield (military / defense). → [`Icons.md`](Icons.md)
- **The desk UI:** parchment ledgers, clipboards, filing trays, wax seals, and a
  gold-engraved title plate. Buttons are stamps; confirmations are seals; the
  resource bar is the realm's balance sheet. → [`UI.md`](UI.md)
- **The splash template:** glory above, paperwork below, golden god-light binding
  them. → [`Moodboards.md`](Moodboards.md)

## Palette shorthand

Warm parchment neutrals as the base, then: **royal blue + warm gold** (player /
civic / Order), **heraldic red** (military / defense), **warm gold + coin**
(economy / currency), **radiant warm light** (divine / faith). One dominant hue +
one accent per visual; everything else recedes. No neon, no rainbow, no
oversaturation. (Full discipline: style guide.)

## World facts that constrain art

Pre-industrial medieval tech (quills, wax, abacus, windmills, forges — **no**
gunpowder/clockwork/steampunk) · rare reserved magic · an ordered human kingdom
with a treasury, guilds, a quest economy, and a gold (gp) currency · a benevolent
radiant sky-god served by temples · a busy, prosperous, paperwork-laden daily
life.

## Generate-ready checklist

Before shipping any *Asset Report* visual, confirm:

- [ ] It obeys the **style guide** (light, material, edge, palette, silhouette).
- [ ] It reads as **this game** — heroic *and* administrative, warm *and* wry.
- [ ] Category color/banner is correct (civic blue-gold / military red /
      economy gold / divine radiant).
- [ ] Only divine/arcane elements emit light.
- [ ] If it's a building: correct family, Base-Ring furniture, and Top Badge.
- [ ] If it's an icon/resource: correct frame, single concept, legible at 32px,
      reads in grayscale.
- [ ] Pre-industrial medieval; no sci-fi, steampunk, modern-office, or grimdark
      contamination.
- [ ] No baked-in text on the asset; one clear subject; room to breathe.

## What this game is NOT

Not grimdark, not slapstick-cartoon, not sci-fi/steampunk, not modern-corporate,
not photoreal. It is **civilized, sincere, warmly funny high fantasy seen from
the clerk's desk.**
